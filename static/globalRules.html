Global Rules

  1. You must be 18 years or older to use 117chan.
  2. Do not post anything that violates US law.
  3. Do not post, link to, request, or discuss pornographic content involving underage children. (This will result in a permanent ban, which cannot be appealed).
  4. Do not flood or spam.
  5. Do not post furry, pony/brony, or loli/shota images/content.
  6. Do not evade bans (this may result in a permanent ban), instead appeal your ban.
  7. Advertising is not welcome--this includes referral linking, "offers", soliciting, begging, etc.
  8. Do not post adult content on safe for work (sfw) boards.
  9. Do not impersonate 117chan staff members.
