Thanks for expressing interest in becoming a volunteer at 117chan!

As a board janitor you will be in charge of maintaining order, and enforcing both global and board rules.

Janitor's CAN...
  1. Removing threads, replies and images that pertain to their assigned board(s).
  2. Submit ban/warning requests to moderators.
  3. Access the report log.

Janitor's CANNOT...
  1. Ban users.
  2. Use role identifiers, or set rules.

Requirements
  1. Be a responsible individual.
  2. Be able to use IRC/Mumble, and be regularily contactable.
  3. Have a deep understand of established rules.
  4. Be accountable, all of your actions are logged.
  5. Remain annonymous, do not disclose your role.

To apply, please send an email to admin@117chan.org [admin (at) 117chan.org]. Please make the subject of the email "Janitor Application." In the body of the email please include:

  1. Name
  2. Age
  3. Best email to reach you
  4. Timezone
  5. How active are you on 117chan?
  6. Board you're applying to be a Janitor for
  7. Your qualifications
  8. Issues that are currently present on the board you are applying to?
