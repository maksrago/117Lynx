What is 117Chan?

117chan (also known as Incelchan, or RobotChan) is an imageboard for male virgins, non-normies, and social outcasts where they can express their feelings, and discuss their lifestyles. 117chan is influenced from similar imageboards such as Wizardchan, Warlockchan, and Virginchan.

  1. Virgin: a person who, in his entire life, has never engaged in intimate acts of vaginal sex, oral sex, anal sex, mutual masturbation, or other forms of non-penetrative sex, with another person
  2. Normie: a person who does not have a mental illness such as bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, mood disorders, PTSD, depression or any similar mental disability. "Normie" is a reference to those who are a part of the mainstream culture; the 97% of the population who do not have a mental illness.
  3. Social Outcast: someone who is rejected from the majority, or all of the social groups in an area.

If you are not one or more of these things then your probably don't belong here, click here to be redirected to a more fitting place. Please be advised, succubi (or women) are not allowed on this website.

How does 117chan work?

117chan is running off of the LynxChan backend, and the 117chan fron-end, a fork of PenumbraLynx. All of the software used to create this website is libre, and respects your privacy/freedom. Analytics are gathered using a tool called Matomo (formarly known as Piwik).

Full IP addresses are not logged, only the first two segments are, in order to prevent spamming. We do not use Google's captcha system, nor do we use any Google integrations. For more information on how 117chan operates visit the about page.

How do I format text?

Tags

  1. [spoiler]the game[/spoiler] -> the game
  2. **this also works** -> this also works
  3. ==SRZ BIZNIZ== -> SRZ BIZNIZ
  4. ''italics'' -> italics
  5. '''bold''' -> bold
  6. __underline__ -> underline
  7. ~~strikethrough~~ -> strikethrough
  8. [aa] for japanese characters
  9. [code] tags if enabled by board owner

Quote

If you write >> and then a number to a posting, it will create a link to this post in the board you are posting and add a tooltip with the post preview if you have javascript enabled.

Example:
    >>50 -> >>50

Board link

If you write >>> and then add a board name between slashes, it will create a link to the board.

Example:
  1. >>>/cancer/ -> >>>/cancer/

Cross-quote

It is also possible to quote posts from another boards. Just add the posting number after the board name.

Example:
  1. >>>/cancer/50 -> >>>/cancer/50

Greentext

If you add a > in the start of a line, it will greentext the text until it finds a new line.

Example:
  1. >le ebin upboat :^) -> >le ebin upboat :^)

Urls

If you post a url starting with either http or https, it will automatically be linked.

Example:
  1. http://bot.net -> http://bot.net

What is Sage?

Typing "sage" into the email field when replying to a post will generate a reply without bumping the thread.

What is a tripcode?

By default posts are made anonymously on 117chan. However you may use the "name" field to establish an identity.

  1. To use a tripcode, add a # in your name. After the #, add the password for the tripcode.
  2. The server will apply a hash to the password and display the hashed password.
  3. To use a secure tripcode, add ## in your name and a password. A secure tripcode will generate a random text to be displayed that will only be used if your password matches.

What is a role signature?

If you wish to display your global or board role when posting, you can put #rs in your name, like a tripcode, case insensitive. This will prevent you from using a tripcode. You can also enable an always use role signature rule in your account settings.

Can I appeal a ban?

Yes, get in contact with a moderator on either IRC, Mumble, or email ban@117chan.org [ban (at) 117chan.org].

How can I become a volunteer?

Typically if volunteers are needed a global announcement will be sent out. The necessity for volunteers may vary from being a board janitor, a 117chan developer, IRC/Mumble moderator etc.
