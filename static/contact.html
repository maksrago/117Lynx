DMCA Takedowns

If you are a copyright holder of an image/video/file and would like it removed send a PROPERLY-FORMATED DMCA Takedown Notice to legal@117chan.org [legal (at) 117chan.org].

For legal reasons, DMCA Takedown Notices MUST include the following:

  1. Name or Company Name
  2. Address
  3. Daytime telephone
  4. Email address
  5. Name or title of Work(s) to be removed
  6. URLs of offending files or posts

In addition, please note that all DMCA take downs are publically logged and availble to users of the site for transparency purposes. If you are a user and would like to view previous DMCA takedowns click here.
